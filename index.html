Overview

Gogotan, LLC is a Colorado-based staffing firm. We provide workforce staffing and related services to businesses that contract with us across a range of service industries.

Industries we staff

  • Restaurant and food service
  • Beverage and alcohol service
  • Cleaning and janitorial
  • Event management
  • Coaching
  • Other service sectors on request

How it works

  1. A business contracts with Gogotan for its staffing needs.
  2. Gogotan recruits, employs, and assigns qualified workers to meet those needs.
  3. Assigned staff perform the agreed work for the duration of each engagement.
