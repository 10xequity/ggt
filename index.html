Turnkey staffing for businesses of every size, based in Colorado. Over a decade of experience.

One contract. A complete workforce.

Most companies don't have a hiring problem. They have an employment problem. Recruiting, onboarding, payroll, HR, and compliance add up to a full-time operation before anyone does a minute of actual work.

Gogotan absorbs all of it. Your company contracts with us for the roles you need, whether that's one position or an entire team, for a season or for good. We recruit the talent, employ them as W-2 employees or engage them as 1099 contractors depending on what your situation calls for, and manage everything that comes with it:

  • Recruiting, screening, and placement
  • Payroll and tax administration
  • HR management and employee relations
  • Onboarding, scheduling, and workforce coordination
  • Fractional management when you need leadership too

To be clear: Gogotan is not a recruitment company. Recruiters find you a candidate, collect a fee, and hand you the employment burden. The people working in your business work through Gogotan. No new headcount on your books, no payroll setup, no HR department required. One relationship, one invoice, a fully managed workforce.
